## Launch Objective

Vanguard Heavy carries the fourth orbital array module required to extend AEON's deep-space observation lattice. The payload includes sensor calibration hardware and relay infrastructure.

## Readiness Notes

Vehicle integration remains within tolerance and weather outlook is stable. Final rehearsals are focused on payload deployment synchronization once the stack reaches insertion altitude.

## Why It Matters

Module 4 closes a key coverage gap in AEON's outer-observation network, improving response time for mission telemetry across the agency's deep-space assets.
