## Launch Objective

Ares Interceptor delivers AEON's next-generation Mars rover with upgraded autonomous hazard routing and subsurface thermal drills.

## Readiness Notes

The launch stack is in final avionics checkout. Teams are validating surface relay compatibility with the current Mars orbital communications mesh.

## Why It Matters

The rover is designed to work the margins between exploration and infrastructure, identifying future crew-safe traverses while also evaluating in-situ resource extraction prospects.
