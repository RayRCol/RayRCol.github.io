## Competition Overview

Participating in a Kaggle competition centered on predicting patient outcomes from clinical tabular data. The challenge involves feature engineering, handling missing clinical measurements, and tuning ensemble classifiers for a highly imbalanced target variable.

## Preparation

Model development began with a baseline XGBoost pipeline. Current experiments are testing stacked ensembles combining LightGBM, CatBoost, and neural networks with feature selection via permutation importance.

## Target

Top 10% placement on the final private leaderboard, with a stretch goal of a bronze medal. The primary learning objective is building robust cross-validation strategies for medical tabular data.
